Woof! I'm Luke.

February 27, 2014

Today, I found my forever home! There is lots of room and I can sleep wherever I want. I had an accident on the carpet and momma wasn't too happy, but I promise I will learn to go in the right spot! I sleep a lot and momma loves to cuddle with me.

My favorite time of day is meal time! It happens three times a day and it makes my tummy so happy. After meal time, I play with my toys then take a nap. When I sleep, I dream of running around a large field and playing with other doggies. Momma says my dreams are so vivid that I actually move my legs in my sleep.

My First Play Date

April 20th, 2014

Today, momma took me to the dog park for the first time. I had no idea there were so many other doggies in the world! I had so much fun meeting new friends and chasing them around.
